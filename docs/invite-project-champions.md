# Be a Project Champion at the Gies AI For Impact Challenge

**April 23-24, 2026 | Business Instructional Facility**

We're hosting the first **Gies AI For Impact Challenge** ([go.gies.illinois.edu/ai-challenge](https://go.gies.illinois.edu/ai-challenge)) — a 24-hour event where 80-120 Gies students will build AI agents that automate common business workflows using Microsoft Copilot Studio. We'd love your involvement.

## How to Participate

**Propose a Challenge** — Suggest a business process that would benefit from automation. It doesn't need to be specific to your actual systems — frame it as a common business scenario (e.g., "streamline an employee onboarding workflow"). Students benefit from the realism, and you get to see how automation could apply to work like yours.

**Mentor Student Teams** — Be available as a domain expert. Students building a finance automation need to talk to someone in finance. Your real-world perspective is valuable even without a formal challenge.

**Or both** — Propose a challenge and mentor the teams working on it.

We have five tracks:

| Track | Example Challenges |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Finance & Accounting** | Invoice processing, expense management, financial reporting |
| **HR** | Candidate screening, onboarding, employee inquiry routing |
| **Marketing & Sales** | Campaign management, content generation, analytics automation |
| **Business Technology** | System integrations, IT service automation, data pipelines |
| **General Innovation** | Any operational process you wish ran itself |

## Submit Your Challenge

Champions from **Marcom**, **Magelli Office of Experiential Learning**, **SCC**, and the **Office of the Dean** have already submitted challenges — from automating prospect identification to transforming assessment data into decision-ready insights. We want yours too.

**Two ways to submit:**

1. **[Chat with the Challenge Builder](https://champion-chat-swart.vercel.app)** (recommended) — an AI-powered conversation that asks about your work, shows you what Copilot Studio can do with examples relevant to your area, and helps you shape a challenge. Takes about 5 minutes. You can ask it questions — treat it like a conversation, not a form.

2. **[Fill out the Google Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfzArV3W-cSYe_cdO121pwrBg3Ar4HvfE6YlWj7JMHLMU0A4g/viewform?usp=header)** — if you already know what you want to submit and prefer a traditional form.

## About the Data: Nothing Real, Nothing Live

**No real Gies data, systems, or integrations are involved.** All student projects use simulated or synthetic data. The challenges are framed as realistic business scenarios, not projects tied to your internal processes. Zero operational risk.
