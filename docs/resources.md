## Judging Rubric

Projects will be evaluated on:

| Criteria | Weight | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **Business Impact** | 30% | Real-world applicability and value creation |
| **Innovation** | 25% | Creativity and novel approaches to workflow automation |
| **Technical Execution** | 20% | Functionality, reliability, and user experience |
| **Presentation** | 15% | Clarity of communication and demonstration quality |
| **Scalability** | 10% | Potential for broader adoption and extension |
